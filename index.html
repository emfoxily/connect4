How to Play

The best game in the wizarding world since Wizard's Chess! Represent your Hogwarts house in this revamped version of Connect Four! Each player will select their house and the first player that does so, makes the first move. Click any empty circle on the board to place your house crest. Whoever gets four in a row vertically or horizontally first, wins! Click the button below to unlock the game!
